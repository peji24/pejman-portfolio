About Me

My name is Pejman Bidad. My major is Mechanical Engineering, Master of Science degree obtained from University of Houston, TX, USA in January 2016.

I can describe myself as a person who enjoys to try different adventurous things in life both in science and in everyday life; That clearly justifies why I have done different multidisciplinary research projects and jobs related to Energy and related topics. Alongside that, I was recently drawn to some other scientific projects and categories in Machine Learning and Data Science using Python which I can see obvious links between Engineering and other state-of-the-art researches like neuroscience and neural networks demanding to combine various skills to increase precision, lowering errors and exploring more ambiguous aspects of research as it is .

Beyond all of those, As an engineer and when I used to go to gyms to workout, I found similar connections between static/dynamic mechanical rules and human bodies anatomy. And this pushed me to study more about  such criteria and I achieved the most respected and reputable degree of fitness and training from NASM in the USA.
